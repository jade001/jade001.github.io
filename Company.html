Our Company

JADE Asset-Based Lending Services Incorporated

JADE ABL is a Canadian company specialized in Field Examinations for Asset Based Lending financing. The company was co-founded by Carmie Cammelleri and Lucy Conte in 2013 serving the Greater Montreal Area.

It has been accredited by a number of Canadian and US Lenders to conduct field examinations since 2013.

In 2015, Allen Bacarro joined the company serving the Greater Toronto Area and the rest of Canada.
