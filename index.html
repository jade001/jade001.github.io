Expertise, Experience
and Commitment to Excellence

The Partners of JADE ABL gained their expertise through years of conducting field examinations
while working directly with ABL lenders and as independent examiners.
Exposure to various manufacturing, trading and other industries provide the JADE ABL partners
with vast knowledge and insights which enable them to add value to a field examination engagement.

Value Added Service

As part of its standard field examination procedures in examining the Borrowing Base, JADE ABL aims to leverage its expertise, experience and industry knowledge by providing insights on the Borrower’s operations and the Borrower’s relationships with their customers and suppliers.

Over the years, the Partners developed analytical tools and techniques to effectively and efficiently conduct the examination, document the procedures and report the results.

Presenting information in an organized, brief and concise fashion is key to making a report readable. JADE ABL has the experience to sift through loads of information, separate the trivial and highlight areas of concern to the various readers of the report.
